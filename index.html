Our Facilities

An integral part of our success is our state-of-the-art facilities — purpose-built environments where every thoroughbred gets every possible chance to reach their full racing potential.

In 2021 McEvoy Mitchell Racing opened their fully custom-designed complex directly on course at the Ballarat Turf Club — built from the ground up with one objective: giving every horse in the stable the best possible environment to thrive.

The complex features 70 boxes, two large 20-horse undercover walkers with a polytrack surface, two treadmills, and an adjoining swimming pool. Being on course gives every horse direct access to the outstanding tracks and facilities of the Ballarat Turf Club without ever leaving the precinct.

Flemington Flemington Training Precinct

1 Covered Walker

Grass Gallops Undercover Walker Jump Outs Access Beach Access Metro Environment Trial Track

In 2025 the operation expanded into a 32-box stable within the prestigious Flemington training precinct — one of the country's leading racing centres. Immediate access to grass gallops, official trials, and jump outs at Flemington Racecourse gives horses a metropolitan edge.

Training from Flemington also gives horses easy access to the beach — invaluable for fitness, recovery, and variety in their work. Exposure to a world-class metropolitan environment prepares every horse for the biggest stages in Australian racing.

How It Works

The McEvoy
Selection Method

Tony and Calvin put a huge amount of work and research into the sales — on a mission to uncover the next Sunlight or Coco Sun. By the time the team decides to bid, every horse has been through a rigorous four-step process.

Hours invested in catalogue study, pedigree research and pre-sale inspections at stud farms. Nothing is left to chance.

Our Intelligence Partner

Belmont Bloodstock

The McEvoys combine their expertise with Belmont Bloodstock — an independent agency owned and run by Damon Gabbedy — to ensure every horse of interest is seen at the busiest sales. His assistant Lauren Fairless provides in-depth pedigree reports on every shortlisted horse.

The Four Step Playbook

Every Horse. Every Time.

01
I

Inspect

We carefully assess each horse's physique, movement, and temperament. Every horse we bid on is inspected at least three times before we commit.

02
II

Pedigree

Damon Gabbedy and Lauren Fairless from Belmont Bloodstock provide an in-depth pedigree report — bloodlines, siblings, sire statistics, and breeding trends.

03
III

Ratings

Our analytical team provides racetrack performance figures on the horse's entire family — going back generations to identify patterns of success.

04
IV

Veterinary

Concept Equine Veterinary inspects every horse and undertakes x-rays, scopes and full examination. Strict pass or fail. No exceptions.

The Head Coach

Tony McEvoy

HEAD COACH · McEvoy Racing · Adelaide & Melbourne

Tony McEvoy is one of Australia's most respected thoroughbred trainers, operating dual stables at Morphettville in South Australia and Flemington in Victoria. With over two decades of experience at the highest level, his patient, horse-first approach has produced more than 750 winners including multiple Group 1 champions across every state.

A meticulous student of the breeding and race-placing, Tony's ability to identify the right race for the right horse at precisely the right time sets his operation apart. Owners enjoy full transparency, regular communication, and the expertise of a team that genuinely cares about every horse in the stable.

Hear From The Squad

Real people. Real careers. Real talk about life at McEvoy Racing.

06 Track Rider
Ben Thompson With the stable 7 years

"I grew up riding at a small country track. I never thought I'd be working Group 1 horses at Morphettville. Tony gave me a shot and I haven't looked back."

Ben started as a casual track rider at 19 and worked his way to Senior Track Rider. He now rides 4–6 horses every morning and is Tony's go-to for the nervous young horses that need a calm, confident pair of hands.

07 Senior Strapper
Lisa Chen With the stable 6 years

"The horses are everything. When Bella Nipotina won the Goodwood, I cried. I had been with her every single morning for two years. That win was mine too."

Lisa came to McEvoy Racing straight from a horse welfare background. She now manages the full daily care of six horses at Flemington — grooming, feeding, health monitoring, and race-day preparation. The horses love her.

08 Trackwork Rider
Ryan Doyle With the stable 4 years

"My mates are sitting in offices. I'm on a Group 1 horse at Flemington at 5am watching the sun come up. I genuinely cannot believe this is my job."

Ryan finished his apprenticeship at 20 and joined McEvoy Racing because of the calibre of horse. Four years in, he rides 3–4 horses every morning and has become one of Tony's most trusted trackwork riders for the younger horses.
